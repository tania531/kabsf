Our mission is to educate, connect and inspire to bring positive personal and social change through the ancient wisdom of Kabbalah.

For almost 20 years Bnei Baruch San Francisco has been an authentic Kabbalah study center for people of all backgrounds and levels of advancement. Free live online interactive courses are taught by Bnei Baruch certified instructors to help new students learn foundations of authentic Kabbalah. Check out Kabbalah Education Center website for more information and schedules.
